Classification of drone on basis of its size and weight
  1. Nano Drones

    2. The drones measure around 16 × 2.5 cm (6 × 1 in) and provides troops on the ground with local situational awareness. They are small enough to fit in one hand and weigh 18 g (0.7 oz), with batteries. they can weigh upto 250 g.

  2. Micro Drones

    3. They weigh less than 2 kg and more than 250 grams.They have wingspan less than 50cm.

  3. Small drones

    4. They weigh between 2kg to 25kg.They have wingspan less than 50cm to 2m.

  4. Medium drone

    5. They weigh more than 25 kg and less than 150 kg and their wing dimensions are between 5m to 10m.

  5. Large drone

    6. They weigh more than 150 kg and their wingspan dimensions are more than 10m
